about me & work links

Hi! I'm Tallulah, she/her. I'm a designer and programmer based out of Minneapolis, MN, although I'm originally from the UK! I studied Computer Science at Beloit College, and have been working in Apple retail since then. Most of my free time is taken up by reading, playing video games, and trying to find time to work on the elusive "personal projects".

My favourite games are Horizon Zero Dawn, Psychonauts, and Breath of the Wild, and I love more text based puzzles and stories like Return of the Obra Dinn and Disco Elysium. When I'm not rereading my favourite Robin Hobb books, I'm searching out new fantasy and sci fi books, most recently the Broken Earth Trilogy by N.K. Jemisin.

Currently looking forward to enjoying the Minnesota summer lakeside, and am interested in work in either game design, user interface design for games, or user experience design and research in general. Comfortable with contact through email at tallulahshepherd@gmail.com and LinkedIn.
